Ms. Clare Quigley

Consultant Eye Surgeon

About

Clare Quigley is a Consultant Eye Surgeon in private practice in Progressive Vision, Dublin, providing a comprehensive ophthalmic service, including cataract and oculoplastic surgery. Her professional goal is to deliver a top-quality patient-centered service.

Specialist Expertise

Ms. Clare Quigley specializes in cornea and external eye diseases, with extensive training and experience in ophthalmic surgery. She provides comprehensive eye care with particular expertise in cataract surgery and oculoplastic procedures.

Working alongside a team of skilled professionals at Progressive Vision, Ms. Quigley ensures patients receive thorough assessments and personalized treatment plans in a comfortable and modern clinical environment.
