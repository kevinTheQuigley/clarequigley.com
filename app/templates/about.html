About Me

Margery Stapleton. Artist.

Currently based in Limerick, I am an oil painter who finds endless inspiration in the dynamic beauty of the sea. My work is a tribute to the raw power and subtle grace of moving water, particularly in its most untamed forms. Whether capturing the ferocity of rough seas or the quiet intimacy of a wave gently meeting the shore, my paintings strive to evoke the deep emotional connection we share with the ocean.

Each brushstroke is an exploration of the ocean's dual natureâ€”both a source of tranquility and a force of immense power. I seek to create pieces that resonate on a personal level, inviting the viewer to feel the pull of the tides and the energy of the water as if they were standing at the water's edge.

My artistic journey is one of continual discovery, where every canvas becomes a vessel for exploring the infinite moods of the sea. Through the vivid textures and rich tones of oil paint, I aim to capture moments that are both ephemeral and timeless, inviting you to experience the profound beauty and mystery of the ocean as I see it.
